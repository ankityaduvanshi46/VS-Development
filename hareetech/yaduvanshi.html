Here is a large summary on **Artificial Intelligence (AI):** ### Artificial Intelligence (AI) – Comprehensive Summary Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on creating systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding language, recognizing patterns, and making decisions. AI has evolved from a theoretical concept into one of the most transformative technologies of the 21st century. #### History and Development The idea of intelligent machines dates back centuries, but modern AI began in the 1950s. Researchers believed computers could simulate human intelligence through algorithms and logical reasoning. Early AI systems were limited by computing power and data availability. With advances in hardware, the internet, and massive datasets, AI experienced rapid growth in the 2000s and 2010s. #### Types of AI AI can be classified into three major categories:

1. **Narrow AI (Weak AI)** – Designed for specific tasks such as voice assistants, recommendation systems, and image recognition. 2. **General AI (Strong AI)** – A theoretical form of AI capable of performing any intellectual task that a human can do. 3. **Superintelligent AI** – A hypothetical future
AI that would surpass human intelligence in nearly every field. * **Machine Learning (ML):** Systems learn from data and improve over time. * **Deep Learning:** Uses neural networks inspired by the human brain. * **Natural Language Processing (NLP):**
Enables computers to understand and generate human language. * **Computer Vision:** Allows machines to interpret visual information. * **Robotics:** Combines AI with physical machines to perform real-world tasks.
