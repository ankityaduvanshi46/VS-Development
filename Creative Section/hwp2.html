3. About Facebook

Facebook is one of the most popular social media platforms in the world. It was launched in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and his college friends. People use Facebook to connect with family and friends.
Users can share photos, videos, and status updates. It allows people to send messages instantly. Facebook also lets users make voice and video calls. People can join groups based on their interests.
Businesses use Facebook to advertise their products and services. Many organizations use it to share important news and updates. Users can like, comment, and share posts with others.Facebook helps people
stay connected across different countries. It also provides entertainment through videos and live streams. The platform supports online communities and discussions. Facebook offers privacy settings to protect
user information. It is available on both computers and smartphones. Millions of people use Facebook every day. It has become an important part of modern communication. Facebook helps people learn about current events and trends. It brings people together from different cultures and backgrounds. Overall, Facebook is a useful platform for communication, learning, and entertainment.
