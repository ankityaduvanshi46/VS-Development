A sanctuary of elegance and joy in Jabalpur

Vasundhara Resort is nestled near the iconic Bhedaghat and Marble Rocks of Jabalpur. Our sprawling 15-acre property features lush green lawns, luxurious accommodations, an award-winning restaurant, and premier event venues.

Whether it's a romantic getaway, family vacation, destination wedding, or high-energy pool party, Vasundhara offers an unforgettable blend of serenity and celebration.
